Predictive Inventory Management System for E-Commerce Sellers Using AI

The "AI-Powered Inventory Forecasting System" is a Final Year BCA Project designed to demonstrate the application of Artificial Intelligence in E-Commerce logistics.

Objectives

  • To analyze historical sales data and identify trends.
  • To predict future product demand using machine learning algorithms.
  • To help retailers optimize stock levels and reduce storage costs.
  • To provide actionable insights through a visual dashboard.

Problem Statement

E-commerce sellers often face challenges with inventory management. Overstocking ties up capital and storage space, while understocking leads to lost sales and dissatisfied customers. Traditional manual methods are error-prone and inefficient.

The AI Solution

This project utilizes Linear Regression, a fundamental machine learning algorithm, to forecast demand. By analyzing past sales patterns, the system calculates a probable demand figure for the upcoming period, allowing sellers to prepare in advance.
